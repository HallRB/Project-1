Can you survive a day in 2020?

In this choose your own adventure game, you will navigate through the 2020 hellscape at the beginning of THE CRISIS!


Your Goal:

Reach the end of the adventure within the time limit, without getting infected, or letting your panic meter fill up.


To begin the game, press "Start". As you progress through the story, you will be given choices to determine your actions. Choices may carry unseen consequences including time lost, panic gained, or infection. They will result in a number of failed endings. You will lose if the timer runs out, the panic meter is filled, or you've made a decision that's caused you to be infected. Make it to the end safe and quickly to survive.
